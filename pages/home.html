Welcome To BPIT

The institute is situated at a prime location on Dr. K.N. Katju Marg, which separates Sector 11 and Sector 17 in Rohini, North Delhi. Adjacent to BPIT is the MCD Building, which houses Railway Reservation Centre. It is well connected with public transport system like DTC buses and metro shuttles. Rithala metro station on metro line 1 (Red Line) and the Rohini Sector-18/19 metro station on metro line 2 (Yellow Line) are at a distance of 4.6 km & 4.3 km respectively.
